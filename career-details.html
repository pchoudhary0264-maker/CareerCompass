Software Engineer

📖 About

Software Engineers design, develop, test, and maintain computer software, websites, and mobile applications. They solve real-world problems using technology.

📚 Required Subjects

Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science

🎯 Skills Required

Problem Solving, Programming, Logical Thinking, Teamwork

🏫 Popular Colleges in India

IITs, NITs, IIITs, BITS Pilani, Delhi Technological University

💼 Career Opportunities

Software Developer, Web Developer, App Developer, AI Engineer, Data Scientist

💰 Average Salary

₹4–12 LPA (can increase with experience)

📈 Future Scope

Technology is growing rapidly, making Software Engineering one of the most in-demand careers worldwide.
